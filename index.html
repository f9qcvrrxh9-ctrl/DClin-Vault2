Tags: Use the top icons to categorize notes (CBT, Neuro, etc). The background shifts to match your choice.
Filter: Tap icons below the search bar to filter by category. Use ALL to see everything.
Search: Type in the search bar to find specific keywords within your notes.
Export: Tap the download icon in the search bar to save your current filtered list as a .txt file.
Calendar: Blue dots show added notes; Red dots show due dates. Tap a day to see its tasks.
