How To Use
System updates or clearing your browser cache will wipe this app's data. Back up your log frequently!
1. Backup
Tap 'Download Full Log Backup' to save your data as a secure .json file. Click "More" and select "Save to Files" to store it securely.
2. Restore
If your app clears, tap 'Restore Log from Backup', select that saved .json file, and your data will instantly return.
