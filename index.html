How To Use
Clinical Governance & Confidentiality
This is a personal training log. Do not enter sensitive, identifiable patient information into this app. Always maintain standard HCPC/BPS confidentiality guidelines when reflecting on clinical work.
System updates or clearing your browser cache will wipe this app's data. Back up your log frequently!
1. Backup
Tap Download to save your data as a secure .json file. Click "More" and select "Save to Files" to store it securely.
2. Restore
If your app clears, tap Restore, select that saved .json file, and your data will instantly return.
Tags Use icons to categorize notes.
Filter & Search Filter by category or keyword. Tap download icon to export text.
Calendar Filter lists by day. Overdue items highlight in red. Use settings to set Amber warnings.
Extra Learning Log Track independent study. Set progress goals in the settings.
Professional Journal A dual-stream chronological ledger to record activity and supervisor feedback as proof of work.
Expenses Set Base Postcode and £/mile rate in settings. Auto-calculates mileage via API. Use Quick-Fill to save frequent locations.
