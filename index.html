How To Use
System updates or clearing your browser cache will wipe this app's data. It is highly recommended to back up your vault frequently!
1. Backup
Tap 'Download Full Vault Backup' to save your data as a secure .json file. Click "More" and select "Save to Files" to store it securely on your device.
2. Restore
If your app clears, tap 'Restore Vault from Backup', select that saved .json file, and your data will instantly return.
Tags Use the top icons to categorize notes (Reflection, Research/Thesis, etc). Tagging notes makes them much easier to filter and find later in the Lists section.
Filter & Search Tap icons below the search bar to filter by category, or type to find specific keywords. Tap ALL to see everything. Tap the download icon in the search bar to export your currently filtered list as a .txt file.
Calendar Tap a day to filter your lists. The calendar elegantly displays both tasks created on that date (white dots) and tasks with deadlines on that date (colored dots matching their tag). Upcoming deadlines will dynamically appear below the calendar.
Extra Learning Log Keep track of your independent study. Select the activity type, date, and hours, then add your reflection. Exporting this will instantly generate a pre-formatted file that perfectly matches your university spreadsheet columns for easy copy-pasting.
Expenses Tap the gear icon in the Expenses section to set your Base Postcode and £/mile rate. Your base will auto-fill as the 'From' location to save time. It calculates mileage automatically via public API and can be exported as a clean spreadsheet.
