How To Use
System updates or clearing your browser cache will wipe this app's data. It is highly recommended to back up your vault frequently!
1. Backup
Tap 'Download Full Vault Backup' to save your data as a secure .json file. Click "More" and select "Save to Files" to store it securely on your device.
2. Restore
If your app ever clears unexpectedly, tap 'Restore Vault from Backup', select that saved .json file, and your data will instantly return exactly as it was.
Tags Use the top icons to categorize notes (CBT, Neuro, etc). Tagging notes makes them much easier to filter and find later in the Lists section.
Filter & Search Tap icons below the search bar to filter by category, or type to find specific keywords. Tap ALL to see everything. Tap the download icon in the search bar to export your currently filtered list as a .txt file.
Links & Sign In Apple limits "Home Screen Apps" for privacy. When clicking the quick links, it may repeatedly ask you to sign into your university Microsoft account. If this is incredibly frustrating, the only fix is to remove the "apple-mobile-web-app-capable" line from the source code, so it opens as a normal Safari tab.
Calendar Tap a day to filter your lists. The calendar elegantly displays both tasks created on that date (blue dots) and tasks with deadlines on that date (red dots).
Expenses Tap the gear icon in the Expenses section to set your Base Postcode and £/mile rate. Your base will auto-fill as the 'From' location to save time, but you can type over it for custom journeys. It calculates mileage automatically and can be exported as a .CSV spreadsheet.
