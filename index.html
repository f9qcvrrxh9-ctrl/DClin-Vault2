How To Use
Clinical Governance & Confidentiality
This is a personal training log. Do not enter sensitive, identifiable patient information into this app. Always maintain standard HCPC/BPS confidentiality guidelines when reflecting on clinical work.
System updates or clearing your browser cache will wipe this app's data. Back up your log frequently!
1. Backup
Tap Download to save your data as a secure .json file. Click "More" and select "Save to Files" to store it securely.
2. Restore
If your app clears, tap Restore, select that saved .json file, and your data will instantly return.
Tags & Filters Use icons to categorize notes. Filter your timeline by tag or search by keyword.
Local Settings (Gear Icons) Each section (Capture, Calendar, Learning, Journal, Expenses) has a gear icon in the top right. Open these to find Export to CSV options, Reflective Framework toggles, Target Hours, and Clear Log buttons.
Reflective Frameworks Enable in Capture, Learning, or Journal settings to auto-inject guided, un-deletable prompts (like Gibbs' cycle) into your text boxes to streamline write-ups.
Postcode Shortcuts In Expense settings, link names like "Uni" to a postcode. Type "Uni" when logging a journey to automatically route the mileage.
Progress Tracking Set target hours in the Extra Learning settings to unlock visual progress rings for your clinical portfolio.
